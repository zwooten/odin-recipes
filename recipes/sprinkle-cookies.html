Sprinkle Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 box of cake mix
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/2 cup of oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • Sprinkles

Directions

  1. Mix all ingredients together in a large bowl
  2. Roll into 1 inch size balls
  3. Put balls of dough onto a baking sheet
  4. Bake for 8 minutes at 350 degrees
