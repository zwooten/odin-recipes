Chocolate Chip Oatmeal Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Mix ingredients together until creamy
  3. Add contents from jar and mix well
  4. Make 2 inch balls and place about 2 inches apart on baking sheet
  5. bake 8 minutes until lightly browned
