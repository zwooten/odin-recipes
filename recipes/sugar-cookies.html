Sugar Cookie Recipe

Description

This is an easy recipe to make the best sugar cookies!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup softened butter
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Sift together the flour, baking powder, salt. Set aside.
  3. Cream together the butter and sugar.
  4. Beat in eggs and vanilla.
  5. Add the flour, salt, baking powder mixture. Mix on low setting until blended.
  6. Roll out the dough into "sheets" between layers of waxed paper.
  7. Cut shapes with a cookie cutter and place on ungreased cookie sheets 1 inch apart. Combine all scraps, roll out again, refrigerate for a few minutes and repeat until all of the dough is gone.
  8. Bake 6-8 minutes (Depending on cookie size) in preheated oven. Cool completely.
